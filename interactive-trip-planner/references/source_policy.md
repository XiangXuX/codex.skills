# Source Policy

## Source Hierarchy

### Transport, hotels, cars, tours

Prefer:
1. Ctrip / Trip.com public booking pages
2. Official operator pages when booking pages are incomplete

### Attractions, passes, rules, entry information

Prefer:
1. Official attraction sites
2. Official tourism boards
3. Official park / transit / ferry pages

## Output Rules

- Always distinguish between public-page observed prices and guaranteed checkout prices.
- Include the research date for volatile information.
- Provide links for user-verifiable facts.
- If inventory pages are unstable or incomplete, say so directly.

## High-Stakes Travel Facts

Always verify when the answer depends on:
- Flight times
- Ferry schedules
- Park passes
- Tour departure times
- One-way rental conditions
- Official fees or entry rules
